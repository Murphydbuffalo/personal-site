Smile-O-Meter

The Smile-O-Meter (under development) is a neural network used to classify images as one of seven emotions. It is written "from scratch", that is, without the use of frameworks such as TensorFlow and Keras. My main goal in working on the Smile-O-Meter was to learn more about machine learning, neural nets, and Python, and to that end it's been a success! In the future I'll be publishing blog posts that describe the architecture and effectiveness of the system.
